Thesis
"Cooking with random ingredients shouldn’t feel limiting
GrumpyChef turns it into a playful, personalized experience powered by AI."

GrumpyChef is an interactive AI-powered recipe generator that transforms culinary exploration into a humorous and engaging experience. Users can submit ingredients through text or images, and a grumpy, sarcastic AI character responds with personalized recipes and witty commentary. The goal of the project was to make cooking more accessible and entertaining, especially when users are unsure what to make with the ingredients they already have. By combining AI-generated recipes with a strong character personality, the experience becomes both functional and memorable.

My exact contribution

Ideation

Design directions explored

Utility-focused recipe generator

An early idea focused purely on generating recipes from ingredients. While useful, it lacked personality and felt similar to existing tools.

Character-driven AI experience ✦

The final direction introduces a sarcastic, grumpy AI persona that delivers recipes with humor and attitude, making the experience more engaging and memorable.

This project was completed in a team of three, combining AI development, backend systems, and interactive front-end design.

Approach

How it was built - step by step

01

Character design & concept

We began by defining the personality of the experience — a sarcastic, grumpy AI chef. This character shaped the tone of the product and guided how users would interact with the system, making humor a core part of the design.

Concept
02

Research & problem framing

We explored how users typically search for recipes and identified frustration when working with limited or random ingredients. This informed the need for a flexible, AI-driven solution.

Research
03

System design & prototyping

We mapped the user flow from ingredient input (text and image) to recipe output, designing how different AI components would interact. Prototypes helped ensure a smooth and understandable experience.

Design
04

AI integration & development

The system uses GPT-4o for ingredient detection and a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline for recipe generation. Flask and FAISS handle backend logic, while DALL·E 2 generates recipe visuals displayed within the interface.

Development
05

Testing & refinement

We refined both the AI responses and the character tone to balance humor with usability, ensuring the experience remained helpful while still feeling playful and engaging.
